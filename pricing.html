Frequently Asked Questions

Will the free dashboard always be free?
Yes. The core dashboard — live events, markets, Kahn scale, and intelligence frameworks — will always be free with no login required. Paid tiers add API access, alerts, and enterprise features.
What conflicts do you cover?
Currently: Iran-USA-Israel 2026. We're building multi-theater support for Ukraine-Russia, Taiwan Strait, and Sudan. Professional and Institutional tiers get early access to new theaters.
How is data verified?
Every fact requires a named source (CENTCOM, Reuters, LSEG, etc.). We use a tiered confidence system: Tier 1 (official military/government), Tier 2 (wire services), Tier 3 (OSINT), Tier 4 (social). Casualty figures require 2+ independent Tier 2+ sources.
Can I embed the widget on my website?
Yes, with Professional or Institutional plans. The embed widget shows key stats, Kahn level, and latest events in a compact format. White-label embedding is available for Institutional clients.
Do you offer academic/NGO discounts?
Yes. Universities, think tanks, and NGOs qualify for 50% discount on Professional and Institutional plans. Contact us with your organization details.
